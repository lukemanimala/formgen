FormGen

The easiest way for creators to generate high-quality 3D music visuals without learning complex tools.

Vision

Upload

  • 3D model
  • Song
  • Style reference
  • Desired mood
  • Duration and format

Generate

  • Interactive browser preview
  • Audio-reactive motion
  • Camera paths
  • Particles, lighting, and effects
  • Final render using cloud GPUs

Why This Matters

Web graphics continue to improve through WebGPU. Cloud GPUs are becoming accessible and affordable.

Most creators don't want to learn Blender, TouchDesigner, Notch, Unreal, shaders, or rendering pipelines. They want results.

Roadmap

Phase 1: Browser Preview

  • Upload GLB model
  • Upload audio
  • Auto-generate camera movement
  • Bass, mid, and treble-driven animations
  • Basic particles, bloom, and trails
  • Export short clips

Phase 2: AI Director

  • Prompt-based styling
  • Multiple visual treatments
  • Automatic camera choreography
  • Beat-aware transitions
  • Genre-specific presets

Phase 3: Cloud Rendering

  • Real-time browser previews
  • High-quality cloud rendering
  • 1080p, 4K, and beyond
  • Advanced simulations and effects
  • Blender, Unreal, or custom pipelines

The goal is not to compete with professional tools.
The goal is to compete with the friction of professional tools.

Creators should achieve 80% of the visual impact with 20% of the effort.

Target Users

Musicians DJs Independent artists Event promoters Content creators Brands Agencies

Initial Validation

Upload a model → Upload a song → Receive a beautiful 15-second music-reactive visual

If that feels magical, the larger vision is worth exploring.
