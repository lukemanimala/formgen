# FormGen Strategy

## The Future-Facing Thesis

A compelling opportunity is not to build another visualizer, but to build **the easiest way for creators to generate high-quality 3D music visuals without learning complex tools**.

---

## Product Vision

### Upload
- 3D model
- Song
- Style reference
- Desired mood
- Duration and format

### Generate
- Interactive browser preview
- Audio-reactive motion
- Camera paths
- Particles, lighting, and effects
- Final render using cloud GPUs when local hardware or browser rendering becomes limiting

---

## Why This Could Matter

Web graphics continue to improve through WebGPU and increasingly capable browser rendering. At the same time, cloud GPUs are becoming more accessible and affordable.

Most creators do not want to learn Blender Geometry Nodes, TouchDesigner, Notch, Unreal Engine, shaders, or rendering pipelines. **They want results.**

The opportunity is to make advanced visual generation accessible through a simple workflow.

---

## Product Roadmap

### Phase 1: Browser Preview
- Upload GLB model
- Upload audio
- Auto-generate camera movement
- Bass, mid, and treble-driven animations
- Basic particles, bloom, and trails
- Export short clips

### Phase 2: AI Director
- Prompt-based styling
- Multiple visual treatments
- Automatic camera choreography
- Beat-aware transitions
- Genre-specific presets

### Phase 3: Cloud Rendering
- Real-time browser previews
- High-quality cloud rendering
- 1080p, 4K, and beyond
- Advanced simulations and effects
- Future support for Blender, Unreal, or custom render pipelines

---

## Core Insight

The goal is not to compete directly with professional tools.

**The goal is to compete with the friction of professional tools.**

Creators should be able to achieve **80% of the visual impact** of high-end software with **20% of the effort**.

---

## Potential Users

- Musicians
- DJs
- Independent artists
- Event promoters
- Content creators
- Brands
- Agencies

---

## Initial Validation

Start with a single magical workflow:

1. Upload a model.
2. Upload a song.
3. Receive a beautiful 15-second music-reactive visual.

**If that experience feels magical, the larger platform vision becomes worth exploring.**

---

## Current State

FormGen currently solves the **model generation** piece:
- Procedural geometry generation with simple controls
- Export to professional tools (Notch, TouchDesigner) via OBJ
- Built-in preview with camera automation and recording

Next step toward the vision: **audio reactivity + GLB import**.
